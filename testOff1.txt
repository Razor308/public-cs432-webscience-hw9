EACE invites Old Dominion University students to go on a Virtual Road Trip to the Real World!

The EACE Road Trips serve as beneficial hybrid workshops that allow for networking opportunities, as well as obtaining information on possible career paths with involved companies.

”Don’t miss your chance to learn first-hand about a career field, network with employees and explore internship and job opportunities.

Virtual site visits for the 2021 Road Trips to the Real World program take place in January 2021 and are $5 per student.

Registration opened November 9 and closes December 31, 2020.

    Visit EACE.org/roadtrips to find out more and to register!
